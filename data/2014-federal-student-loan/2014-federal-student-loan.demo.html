Federal student aid data on Map(2012-13)

This demo shows the average federal award from Federal Work-Study(FWS) at state level on Map. It also includes background information (ie. poverty and population) from US census.

The first two images use data from Campus-Based Volume Reports 2012-13 in Title IV Program Volume Reports, which is published by the office of Federal Student Aid. The last two images use data from U.S. Census Bureau, and data sources are linked right below the figure. Note that state-level statistics are on the location of main campus of school, it does not directly reflect State Residency of the recipients.
